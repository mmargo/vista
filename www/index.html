Help

Welcome to my simple app to explore the ever shrinking planet earth. I have prepared a sample of wonderful photographs for you to enjoy. To play simply view the photograph and choose the location where the photograph was taken.

There are two difficulty levels: Easy and Hard. The Hard level asks you to match the country flag with the background scenery.

Tip: Shake your mobile device to reset your current game
